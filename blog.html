ABSTRACT:

The requirements for the actual usage of artificial intelligence hinge on increased constraints as the needs grow increasingly ambitious and the surge in electricity use prove costly. Based on current research, this translates into about a 30% increase (over time) for electricity use alone and in the coming years is projected to increase.

A simple query ingested into an AI model now requires ten times as much electricity as a normal Google Search.

This raises a huge concern for sustainability as fossil fuels run at a substantial cost. The alternative, among other renewable clean energy sources, is nuclearas; as big business forges full steam ahead with the drastic innovations and developments within the AI space.

The byproducts of such radical change are current hits to employment that result in huge layoffs within the tech industry which currently operates at the detriment of employees who've self-invested with the hope of achieving job security within the very same tech spaces that are now already almost solely driven by artificial intelligence and machine learning models.
